**noiseless channel**
	Nyquist's theorem:$$C = 2B\log_2M$$
		C is the max data rate of the channel (bit/s)
		B is the bandwidth,(Hz)
		M is the number of the level each symbol can represent.

**noise channel**
	Shannon's theorem:$$C = B\log_2(1+\frac{S}{N})$$
		S is the average power of the signal
		N is the average power of the noise
		if use dB, $SNR_{dB} = 10\log_{10}(S/N)$
