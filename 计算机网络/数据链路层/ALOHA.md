
## Pure ALOHA

**methods**
	1. Let users transmit whenever they have data to be sent.
	2. Expected collisions will occur. and distroy the collisions frames
	3. Using a feedback mechanism to know about the status of frame.
	4. Retransmit the destroyed frame

**vulnerable time**
	assume sending a frame take $T_{fr}$ time. then the sending interval is $[t, t+T_{fr}]$, 
	 means other frame can not be sent within $[t-T_{fr}, t+T_{fr}]$.
	 vulnerable time is $2T_{fr}$.
